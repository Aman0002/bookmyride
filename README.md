# 🚖 BookMyRide

**Your Journey. Our Responsibility.**

BookMyRide is a modern intercity cab booking platform designed to simplify travel between cities by providing reliable **Shared Cabs**, **Private Cabs**, and **Airport Transfers**.

The platform initially serves routes connecting **Hisar**, **Delhi**, **Peeragarhi**, and **Chandigarh**, with a long-term vision of expanding into a full-scale mobility platform covering Haryana and North India.

---

## 🌟 Vision

To become the most trusted intercity mobility platform in North India by delivering safe, affordable, and technology-driven transportation services.

---

### Customer Portal
